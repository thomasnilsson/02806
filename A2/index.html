Q5: What is your favorite genre of narrative visualization? Why?

It is difficult to choose because the optimal choice of genre obviously depends on what you are trying to communicate and what data you have available. The same goes for least favorite genre.

Slideshows allows the author to partition the information into separate slides in order to make the information more digestible, i.e. by allowing the reader to go through the information at their own pace. It is easy to move back and forth because of the inherent sectioning of a slide show.

The annotated chart can work very for showing trajectories such as observations related to climate change where annotations may point out events of significance during a time series, such as the industrial revolution. It can also be used to point out how trajectories are unreliable as is the case in Figure 2.

Q6: What is your least favorite genre? Why?
Magazine style; The example given requires the reader to read a large amount of text, which is ideally what the visualisation should convey by itself
